📋 How to Get Your Template Approved by Meta

Follow these rules to avoid rejection and get approved fast.

Template Name
Use only lowercase letters, numbers and underscores. No spaces or special characters.
✅ order_confirmation ✅ welcome_v2 ❌ Order Confirmation ❌ welcome-msg!
Category Selection
Pick the correct category — Meta checks if content matches.
✅ UTILITY — orders, OTPs, reminders ✅ MARKETING — offers, promotions ✅ AUTHENTICATION — login codes ❌ Using UTILITY for pure promotions
Body Text Rules
Be clear and honest. Avoid spam-trigger words and excessive caps.
✅ "Hi {{1}}, your order #{{2}} has been confirmed." ❌ "BUY NOW!!! LIMITED OFFER!!!" ❌ Vague variables like "Click {{1}}" with no context
Variables {{1}}
Variables must be sequential starting from {{1}}. The surrounding text must make the variable's meaning obvious.
✅ "Hello {{1}}, your OTP is {{2}}." ❌ Starting at {{2}} or skipping numbers ❌ Entire body is just "{{1}}"
Footer (Marketing)
For MARKETING templates, always include an opt-out line in the footer. Meta may reject without it.
✅ "Reply STOP to unsubscribe" ✅ "To opt out, reply STOP" ❌ Leaving footer empty on marketing templates
Buttons (Optional)
Max 3 buttons. Button text must match the button action clearly.
✅ Quick Reply: "Yes" / "No" / "Stop" ✅ URL: "Track Order" → your website ❌ URL button with a non-HTTPS link ❌ More than 3 buttons
Fastest Approval: Use UTILITY category with transactional content (orders, appointments, OTPs). Usually approved within minutes.
After Creating: Click Submit to Meta on the template card. Then use Sync from Meta to check approval status.
Auto-Rejected By Meta: Deceptive content, phishing URLs, drug/weapon references, political content, adult content.
